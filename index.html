Lonely Hearts

A solitaire version of Hearts.

Designed for the 2022 Traditional Deck Game Design Contest.

In this solitaire version of Hearts, you will play against the game (The Bot), with the same basic objectives: win tricks, but avoid those with hearts (or the queen of spades*) in them, which carry negative points.

Note: t the moment the queen of spades is NOT special.
